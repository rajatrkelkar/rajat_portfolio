Summary

I help organisations make sense of large, messy, high-stakes datasets — the kind where a wrong number in a regulatory report isn't a UX bug, it's a compliance problem. Most of my work lives at the intersection of Power BI, SQL Server, and governance: building the pipeline, modelling the data correctly, and then making sure the number on the dashboard is one an auditor would sign off on.

Before BI, I spent three years as a business/technical analyst translating ambiguous business needs into specs developers could build from — which is probably why I care so much about documentation, SOPs, and KPI definitions that don't quietly drift over time.
